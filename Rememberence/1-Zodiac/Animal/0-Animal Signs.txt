1) Year of the Rat; [Clever]

1936, 1948, 1960, 1972, 1984, 1996, 2008, 2020, 2032

Rats are the architects of opportunity, quickly turning clever ideas into action. Natural leaders, they thrive in authoritative roles, driven by a relentless ambition that can sometimes appear as self-centeredness. Intellectually sharp and socially savvy, they often excel in business, using their charm and wit to their advantage. While Rats can be talkative, they keep their deeper thoughts private, adding an air of mystery. They are exceptional organizers, making them ideal for roles in management or finance where precision and strategy are key. In relationships, Rats offer loyalty, seeking partners who provide emotional security. Though not overtly romantic, they show love through care and commitment.
