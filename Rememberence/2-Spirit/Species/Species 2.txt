Spirit of a Species

#) Species; [Material]

How are we what we are, is it this flesh? 
Or are we only illusions of ourselves?

------------------------------------
When an item or entity is created, it starts with a number of traits equal to its current tier level and may never have a number of traits beyond its tier level. For instance, a weapon crafted at the Novice tier would have 1 trait. As the item or entity progresses through tiers, it gains an additional trait for each tier level it advances. These may be chosen at random for NPCs with a d6. The same trait can be taken more than once, multiplying the effcts of that trait. Active traits require an amount of MP to activate equal to what ever Biorhythm is controlling the effect, while passive effects are always on if their conditions are met.

1) Avious; [Avium]

Though bird-like, not all can fly. Inquirers of ancient wisdom, and the messengers of fate. The Avious are guardians of the sky, and watchers of the worlds below. Often signs of coming wisdom or change, as moving to new places, be it physical or of the mind. If there is a bird who is out of place and acting unusually, it may be an Avious delivering to you unique wisdom. Dreams of an Avious can denote being overwhelmed if they are flocking you. Alternatively they may represent new wisdom if they appear singularly, or as a guide. Avious of flames, often called the phoenix, represent rebirth and growth. While Avious of death, such as the Crow, represent loss, or the disintegration of old ideals.

Avious are bird-like entities, not all capable of flight, known as inquirers of ancient wisdom and messengers of fate. They are guardians of the sky, observing and influencing the world below. Their presence often heralds wisdom, change, or significant life transitions. An Avious out of place might be delivering unique insights, while dreams of them could signify feeling overwhelmed or receiving new wisdom.

	 Society: Avious serve as omens, guides, and symbols of transformation, embodying the spirit of vigilance, wisdom, and the heralding of new beginnings.

	Events: They might be celebrated in "The Festival of Flight," where their role in guiding and delivering messages is honored, or in rites of passage symbolizing personal transformation.

	Habitat: Prefer high places like mountains or tall trees, symbolizing their connection to the sky and their role as overseers.

	Narrative: Encountering an Avious could represent impending change, the need for wisdom, or the cycle of life and rebirth. It teaches about embracing change, seeking higher knowledge, and understanding the cycles of life and death.

5) Reptoid; [Reptillium]

Cunning to the core, the Reptoids have infiltrated nearly every organization in the known universe. They have no home and thus no strict loyalties. Reptoids are the embodiment of serpentine cunning, infiltrating the fabric of societies with a stealth that belies their presence. 	 Society: Reptoids are the unseen influencers, the manipulators behind the scenes, embodying stealth, adaptability, and cunning. Their existence values secrecy, strategic planning, and the use of power through indirect means, much like how a snake operates in the wild.

	Habitat: Preferring urban environments, political centers, or any place where power and information flow, Reptoids thrive where they can blend in, varied terrains but with an emphasis on society.

	Narrative: Encountering a Reptoid could signify a test of one's own integrity, the need to navigate through deception, or the revelation of hidden truths about power structures, teaching lessons on the nature of power, the importance of vigilance, and the dangers of unchecked ambition.
