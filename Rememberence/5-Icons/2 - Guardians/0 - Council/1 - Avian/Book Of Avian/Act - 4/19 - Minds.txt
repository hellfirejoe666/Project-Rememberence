"Chapter Nineteen"

~Fractured Echoes~


1.	Prologue;
	"With the fall of VoSti, we were left to protect the shards. As the gods vanish into the mysterious realms beyond creation, it is our duty to prevent their power from falling into the wrong hands, and to resist the temptation to use it ourselves. There are others like me, who's stories you must uncover. They act of their own will, and through them you can discover the deeper secrets of Rememberence. With us you can protect the shards, and the realms of Creation, from the forces of Corruption."

2.	Governance;
	"I lie here, a broken husk in this foreign bed, the hum of unseen healers threading through my pain. Every breath burns, a reminder of VoSti’s talons, my ribs grinding like shattered glass. Every moment dancing with shadows of Avelin, Lialin’s tears, the now distant tower, haunting me through silent screams. I fought for Avalon, yet doubt remains: was it enough? The winds within me falter, whispering fear, and doubt, and pain, but I cling to NaFi’s echo, to my own will, anchoring my fractured soul."

3.	Religion;
	"Though I walk through the chambers of the mind, dancing with everything I’ve been and done, I find myself longing for the silent void. My shadow changed me, and the shadow of a God remains stronger still. This darkness was different from the void, a slow and painful drifting through vague shapes and noises. I was gone for so long, unsure if time even existed for me anymore, so long was the excruciating dream. When I finally awaken, would there even be a place for me in Avalon?"
